How do we start projects?

Code for Chicago is focused on making projects shoulder-to-shoulder with our partners.

Start by Listening

At the onset we'll gather info about the problem space with our parners.

Identifying Skills

In our initial scoping we'll identify the technical needs for the project.

Teamwork

Partners are expected to be collaborators throughout the process.

Who leads our teams?

Project and Product Managers lead our teams. They help define the roadmap and create tickets in our kanban board. We'll try to define MVPs and create tasks to get there.

LOREM IPSUM

What does a commitment look like on a weekly basis?

Teams meet weekly for at least 30 min and will either work by themselves or with others throughout the week. We create small tasks with realistic timelines to ensure that volunteers don't feel overwhelmed.

LOREM IPSUM

How long do projects run?

It depends. Expect volunteer projects to run slower than a paid one. Our teams work iteratively to ensure we are thinking through the problem space rather than devising a solution. We aren't our users so we try to do our due diligence through stakeholder and/or user interviews.
